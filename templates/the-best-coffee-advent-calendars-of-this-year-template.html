The Best Coffee Advent Calendars of This Year

January 4, 2024

As the holiday season approaches, coffee lovers rejoice in the anticipation of the perfect advent calendar to count down the days. Here’s a roundup of the best coffee advent calendars for this year, sure to delight any coffee enthusiast:

  1. Starbucks Coffee Advent Calendar - Discover a variety of Starbucks' finest coffees from around the world, each day revealing a new blend or single-origin delight. Perfect for those who love both classic favorites and adventurous new tastes.
  2. Peet's Coffee Advent Calendar - Featuring Peet's signature blends and seasonal favorites, this calendar offers a daily journey through rich, bold flavors and meticulously crafted coffees.
  3. Nespresso Advent Calendar - Indulge in the world of Nespresso with this calendar, featuring a selection of their premium coffee capsules. Each day brings a new espresso or lungo experience, showcasing Nespresso's renowned quality and variety.
  4. Dunkin' Donuts Coffee Advent Calendar - Celebrate the season with Dunkin' Donuts' festive advent calendar, filled with their beloved coffee flavors and limited-edition holiday blends. Perfect for those who enjoy a bit of sweetness in their morning cup.
  5. Independent Roasters' Advent Calendar - For those seeking unique and artisanal coffee experiences, consider advent calendars curated by independent roasters. These calendars often feature a diverse range of single-origin coffees, highlighting the craftsmanship and passion of small-batch roasters.
  6. DIY Coffee Advent Calendar - Embrace creativity by crafting your own coffee advent calendar. Select your favorite coffee beans or ground coffee from different roasters and create a personalized countdown to Christmas. This option allows you to tailor each day’s coffee selection to your specific tastes and preferences.

Whether you prefer the convenience of pre-packaged calendars or the personalized touch of a DIY creation, these coffee advent calendars promise to make your holiday season merry and caffeinated. Enjoy discovering new flavors and celebrating each day with a delicious cup of coffee.
